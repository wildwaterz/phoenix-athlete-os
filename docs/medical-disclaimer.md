# Medical Disclaimer

Version: 1.0
Owner: Kevin Sauvageau
Product: Project Phoenix OS
Status: Living Document

## Purpose

Project Phoenix OS supports recovery and training decisions by organizing information.

It does not provide medical diagnosis or treatment.

## Disclaimer

Project Phoenix OS is not a medical device.

It does not replace advice from:

- Physicians
- Surgeons
- Physiotherapists
- Athletic therapists
- Registered dietitians
- Emergency medical professionals

The app is intended to:

- Track subjective and objective recovery data
- Support better communication with clinicians
- Help the athlete understand trends
- Organize coaching context
- Export structured coach packets

The app should not be used to:

- Diagnose injuries
- Override clinical restrictions
- Make return-to-sport decisions independently
- Ignore concerning symptoms
- Replace emergency medical care

## Clinical Priority

When clinician guidance conflicts with app guidance, clinician guidance wins.

## Red Flag Reminder

The app should encourage users to contact a clinician when they experience concerning symptoms such as:

- Sudden severe pain
- New or worsening calf pain
- Significant swelling increase
- Shortness of breath
- Chest pain
- Fever
- Wound concerns
- Locking or giving way
- Loss of function

## Product Language Rule

Avoid language that implies certainty or prescription.

Prefer:

- “Consider discussing with your physio.”
- “This suggests holding progression.”
- “Informational only.”
- “External coaching makes the final decision.”

Avoid:

- “You are cleared.”
- “You should return to sport.”
- “This diagnosis means…”
- “This treatment will fix…”
